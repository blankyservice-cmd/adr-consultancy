AI Readiness Checklist

20 diagnostic questions to evaluate your business AI readiness, identify quick wins, and estimate ROI potential.

How to use this checklist: Score each item 0 (not at all), 1 (partially), or 2 (fully). Total your score at the end and match it to the readiness band to see where you stand. Be honest -- this is for your benefit.
Dimension 1

Process Readiness

Assess which workflows are candidates for AI automation based on volume, repetition, and error rates.

High-volume repetitive tasks: We have at least one process that runs more than 20 times per week with consistent inputs and outputs.
012
Documented workflows: Our key processes are documented (SOPs, flowcharts, or written guides), not just tribal knowledge.
012
Measurable error rate: We can identify at least one process where human error costs us time, money, or client trust on a regular basis.
012
Time quantified: We know approximately how many hours per week are spent on manual, repetitive tasks (data entry, copy-paste, reporting, etc.).
012
Dimension 2

Data Maturity

Evaluate whether your data infrastructure can support AI -- from collection to quality to accessibility.

Centralized data: Our business data (customer records, transactions, communications) is stored in one or two primary systems, not scattered across spreadsheets and inboxes.
012
Data consistency: Our records follow consistent formats (dates, names, categories) rather than each person entering data their own way.
012
Historical depth: We have at least 6-12 months of historical business data that we could use to train or evaluate AI models.
012
API access: Our primary tools (CRM, accounting, project management) have APIs or integration capabilities (not just CSV exports).
012
Dimension 3

Team Readiness

Gauge organizational appetite for AI adoption and identify potential champions and blockers.

Leadership buy-in: Leadership understands that AI is a strategic priority, not just a tech experiment, and is willing to allocate budget and time.
012
Internal champion: At least one person on the team is genuinely excited about AI and could serve as an internal advocate and point of contact.
012
Change tolerance: Our team has adopted new software or changed workflows in the last 2 years without excessive resistance.
012
Time availability: Key stakeholders can commit 2-4 hours per week during an implementation project for feedback, testing, and validation.
012
Dimension 4

Technology Fit

Map your current tech stack against AI integration requirements and identify gaps.

Cloud-first stack: We primarily use cloud-based tools (SaaS) rather than locally installed legacy software with no integration options.
012
No critical single points of failure: Our operations would not break if we added or replaced a tool, because we are not locked into an unmaintained legacy system.
012
Security baseline: We have basic cybersecurity hygiene in place (MFA on key accounts, password manager, defined access controls).
012
Dimension 5

ROI Potential

Estimate the financial impact of AI across time savings, error reduction, and revenue acceleration.

Labour cost of manual tasks: We can calculate roughly what manual, repetitive work costs us per month in staff time (hours x hourly rate).
012
Revenue leakage identified: We can name at least one area where slow processes, missed follow-ups, or poor visibility is costing us revenue.
012
Scalability bottleneck: We have identified at least one process that forces us to add headcount every time we grow, which AI automation could break.
012

Scoring Guide -- Total: _____ / 40

32-40 (High Readiness): You are well-positioned to implement AI now. Focus on high-impact automations and custom solutions.
22-31 (Moderate Readiness): Strong foundation with specific gaps. We recommend a targeted AI pilot to build momentum while addressing gaps.
12-21 (Building Readiness): Foundational work needed before full AI deployment. Start with a data and process audit.
0-11 (Early Stage): Focus on digitizing and standardizing operations first. AI will amplify what exists -- build the foundation.
